Loyalty Program Selection








Customers earn points for every purchase they make. These points can then be redeemed for rewards such as discounts, free products, or exclusive offers. The more points a customer accumulates, the better the rewards they can unlock



In a tiered loyalty program, customers are segmented into different tiers based on their level of engagement or spending. Each tier offers increasing benefits and rewards. Customers start at a basic tier and can move up to higher tiers by meeting specific criteria, such as spending a certain amount of money within a set period.



This type of loyalty program is simple and straightforward. Customers receive a physical or digital punch card, and each time they make a purchase, they receive a stamp or punch. After collecting a certain number of stamps or punches, they earn a reward.
